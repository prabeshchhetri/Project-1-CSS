About the C9

The C9 Original is the iconic flagship design of the Automoblox system. Featuring sleek curves and a modern silhouette, the C9 blends classic aesthetics with modular creativity. Its signature blue accents make it instantly recognizable and beloved by collectors of all ages.

Features

  • Premium hardwood chassis
  • Signature Automoblox interchangeable parts
  • High-quality wheels and rubberized tires
  • Blue accent detailing unique to the C9
