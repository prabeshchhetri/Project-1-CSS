About the T9

The T9 Pickup Truck is built for strength and utility. With its rugged styling, long wheelbase, and distinctive teal accenting, the T9 stands out as the boldest and most durable design in the Automoblox lineup. Perfect for young builders and collectors who love tough vehicles.

Features

  • Rugged modular body design
  • Large wheel set for off-road look
